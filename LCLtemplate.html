Areas of Influence

Our research is structured around three interconnected pillars that address critical environmental and social challenges.

Pathogens

Investigating the ecological drivers of disease transmission and developing strategies for mitigation.

Ecology

Modeling biodiversity and ecosystem functions to predict environmental change and inform conservation.

Communities

Engaging with local communities through citizen science and co-creation of knowledge.
